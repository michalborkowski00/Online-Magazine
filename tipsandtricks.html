Tips&Tricks

Prevent bubbling over with a wooden spoon

When you put a pot of water on, put a wooden spoon over the top of the pot. If it starts to boil up too high, the spoon will pop the bubbles and keep it from boiling over. It will give you more time to get back and turn the heat down.

You can also use something metal to that, but it will become very hot (not like wood, which doesn't transfer as much heat), so you'd have to remove it with an oven mitt.

Prevent bubbling over with a wooden spoon

Instead of fishing around with your finger or a metal spoon to dig them out of your bowl, use one of the egg shell halves to scoop it out! It is going to stick to the egg shell so you will be able to take it out much easier.
