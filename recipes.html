How to cook cheap meals?

Use less ingredients and look at the ones you already have

It shouldn’t be surprising if we say that meals with fewer ingredients costs less than the ones with the more. In the most cases the dinner with e.g. only five ingredients will costs less than the dinner with ten of them. So the first solution is simple. Just search for a dishes that doesn’t need many products and the required ones aren’t too exotic.

Make a list

It would better to decide on your meals not from day to day, but for example one week in advance. Then, before going to shop, you should make a detailed list of everything that you need. The most important is not to go shopping without a list. One of the reasons is that you may forget something and you would have to go back to the shop. But there is also more important reason – I’m pretty sure that without a list you would buy something that is not necessary, and this is not the best way to save money.

Look at the store brands

People think that the ‘original’ products tastes better than the cheaper ones. But the truth is completely different. It happens very often that the thing that sometimes costs even half of original’s price, will tastes a way better or at least very similar. So there is absolutely no point in paying more for something that only have a brand logo.
