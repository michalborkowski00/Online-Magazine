A little bit about this place

What is it for?

Do you think that you spend too much money on food? Have you just started your new life as a studentand realize that it is not that easy as you thought? Or you simply would like to save some money and spend less on the things on which you can do it? If you are here, it means that you answered ‘yes’ for at least one of these questions or some similar to them.

So how to pay less?

You will find recipes which are made by people all around the world. You can sort them by the price, time of preparation, difficulty or ratings. Moreover, you can read other users opinions and also reviews by our ‘master chefs’. And of course you can add your own food recipes to share them with the others. And we would encourage you to do that!

Also, you can find here the best discounts in groceries. You will find prices of the most common and most used products, and where it would be the best to buy them during the week. Prices are connected with the recipes, so costs of meals will be updated live. That will let you see the actual money you will spend on that.

And how to actually cook?

If you are looking for a video tutorials, you will also find them. Same as some tips&tricks that you can use in the kitchen, during your cooking adventure. Have you ever thought how is it possible to chop onion so fast and precise? Would you like to know how to make your spaghetti looks more like a pasta, not one, big, glued piece? Here you will find out!

What about restaurants?

If you are interested in eating downtown, don’t think that it is impossible without spending huge amounts of money. Here you will find best offers, and restaurants that offer cheap meals, for special occasions, or even without them. If some restaurant or pub, makes a live music, karaoke or dancing evening – you will find it here. Same as with recipes, here you will also find reviews and opinions of other customers.
